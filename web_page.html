Predictive Analytics entails predicting future events with the use of past data analysis and pattern recognition. To predict patterns or behaviours, it uses machine learning and statistical algorithms. However, Prescriptive Analytics goes one step further and makes recommendations for activities to maximise results. It makes targeted suggestions by utilising data, mathematical models, and optimisation approaches. Prescriptive analytics helps decision-makers make the optimal decisions by providing insights to accomplish objectives and enhance overall performance, as opposed to merely speculating about potential outcomes.
